# Find and Replace

## Opening Find

Press `Ctrl+F` (`⌘F`) or go to **Edit → Find**. A panel opens in the top-right corner of the editor.

Type your search query to find matches across all sections in the current song. A counter shows how many matches were found and which one is currently active (e.g. "3 of 7").

## Navigating matches

| Action | Windows/Linux | Mac |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| Next match | `Ctrl+G` or Enter | `⌘G` or Enter |
| Previous match | `Ctrl+Shift+G` | `⌘⇧G` |

The editor scrolls to keep the active match in view.

## Search options

Two toggles are available on the right side of the find bar:

**Aa — Case sensitive**: When on, "Verse" and "verse" are treated as different words.

**Ab| — Whole word**: When on, only whole-word matches are returned. Searching for "the" will not match "there" or "together".

## Replacing text

Press `Ctrl+Shift+H` (`⌘⇧H`) or go to **Edit → Show Replace** to reveal the replace field below the find bar.

- **Replace**: Replaces the currently active match and moves to the next one.
- **Replace All**: Replaces every match in every section at once. This is undoable with `Ctrl+Z` (`⌘Z`).

## Closing Find

Press Escape to close the find panel.
